About me

Hello, I'm Joey, a passionate cartographer and conservationist based in Madison, Wisconsin. I hold a B.S. in Wildlife Ecology and an M.S. in Cartography and GIS, both from the University of Wisconsin–Madison. I specialize in creating visually engaging, data-driven maps that tell the stories of our natural world. My professional background blends hands-on environmental work with cartographic visualization and geospatial analysis, drawing on experience as a field researcher, naturalist, land steward, and GIS technician. You can read more about my work experience here.

In my free time, I love birding, wildlife photography, hiking, rugby, and traveling the world.
