Map of Known Lands

Joseph Willman

My style is called Map of Known Lands and is based on medieval parchment maps.

My map is inspired by medieval parchment maps.

In this map, places are represented by castles, towers, and tents. Highways are represented by real shields.

Scrolling into this map should feel like unrolling a map from the Middle Ages.

Lets take a tour of the places I have lived, starting with my hometown of Grafton, Wisconsin.

Next, Madison where I started as a student in 2020.

Fripp Island, South Carolina where I spent a summer as an intern.

The Adirondack Mountains of New York where I lived this past summer as an intern.

Explore the fields and fortresses on your own.
