Map of Known Lands

This map is inspired by the rich colors and styles of medieval parchment maps. My goal was to create a scrolling experience that feels like delving into a landscape brimming with castles, forts, fields, and farms, evoking a sense of adventure. Cities are represented with symbols of castles, forts, and tents, while highways are adorned with proper shields to maintain the medieval theme. The result is a map that transports viewers to a world reminiscent of the Middle Ages.

Explore the fields and fortresses on your own.
